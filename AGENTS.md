# ReGroup Product Principles

ReGroup is a temporary group coordination app for nights out.

The goal is not tracking.
The goal is awareness.

Users create temporary groups for events such as:
- concerts
- festivals
- nightlife
- bar crawls
- house parties
- city nights

Groups are temporary.

Location sharing is temporary.

The app should answer one question:

"Where is my crew right now?"

Every feature must satisfy at least one of:

- helps friends stay together
- helps friends reconnect
- helps friends get home safely
- improves awareness without increasing surveillance

Avoid:

- social feeds
- posting systems
- followers
- permanent friend networks
- location history
- route replay
- engagement mechanics

Status is more important than exact coordinates.

Awareness is more important than tracking.

Warmth is more important than utility.

ReGroup should feel like:
"We're keeping the crew together."

Not:
"We're monitoring you."
